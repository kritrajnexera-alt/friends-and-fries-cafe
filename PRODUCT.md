# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Locals and visitors in Ankleshwar, Gujarat — people looking for a vibrant social hangout spot with great food, good music, and a welcoming atmosphere. Primarily young adults and friend groups seeking a place to eat, catch up, and unwind.

## Product Purpose

Friends & Fries Cafe is Ankleshwar's favorite hangout — a bold, warm, and social cafe serving loaded fries, smash burgers, cold brews, and desserts at pocket-friendly prices (₹200-400). The website exists to showcase the cafe experience, menu offerings, gallery, and location to attract new customers and give existing regulars a digital home.

## Brand Personality

Bold · Warm · Social

Bold in flavor and visual presence; warm in hospitality and atmosphere; social by design — a place built for conversations and shared moments.

## Anti-references

- No corporate fast-food chain aesthetics (no sterile branding, no plastic feel)
- No generic website templates or stock-feel layouts
- No purple or blue gradients
- No centered hero layouts
- No card-in-card nesting

## Design Principles

1. **Vibe first** — Every page should feel like walking into the cafe: warm lighting, bold accents, and an unmistakable social energy.
2. **Fire and gold** — The D4380D red-orange and FAAD14 golden yellow are the soul of the brand. Use them deliberately, not decoratively.
3. **Unapologetically bold** — Big typography, strong contrast, confident asymmetry. No timidity in layout or hierarchy.
4. **Pocket-friendly, not cheap** — Communicate affordability (₹200-400) without ever looking low-rent. Quality in every pixel.
5. **Local pride** — Ankleshwar is in the name, in the address, in the reviews counter. This is a local joint, not a chain.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Body text maintains ≥4.5:1 contrast (#888 on #0D0D0D is 6.5:1)
- All touch targets minimum 44x44px
- Reduced motion supported via framer-motion's built-in respects
- Semantic HTML structure throughout
